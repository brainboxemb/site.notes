# Feet

This page contains possible feet for the HUB75 display enclosure.

> Prices were checked in July 2026 and may change over time.

## Requirements

The feet should:

- Protect the enclosure and the surface underneath it
- Provide some grip
- Keep the enclosure stable
- Be suitable for mounting underneath the wooden enclosure

## Current Consideration

The Hornbach and Praxis products are inexpensive and have a compact size.

The Bax Music options are dedicated equipment feet and appear more suitable for permanent screw mounting.

The following options are worth comparing first:

- Hornbach TARROX, 20 × 10 mm
- Adam Hall 4903, 20 × 9 mm
- Adam Hall 4900, 25 × 11 mm
- Adam Hall 4906, 38 × 10 mm

The final choice depends on:

- The required clearance underneath the enclosure
- The preferred diameter
- The mounting screw
- Whether the foot needs an internal washer
- The desired appearance
