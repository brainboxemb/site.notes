# Finishing

This page contains options and considerations for finishing the wooden enclosure of the HUB75 LED matrix display.

## Preferred Direction

The current preference is a simple silver-grey wood stain.

The existing display enclosures appear to have a black finish. That works well, but a lighter colour is preferred for the new display because it may be used in direct sunlight.

A silver-grey finish should:

- Be easy to apply
- Require no more than two coats
- Be suitable for the inside and outside of the enclosure
- Provide a lighter finish than black
- Allow some wood grain to remain visible

## Edge Sealing

The sawn edges of the plywood should be sealed before applying the final finish.

- [Hornbach — PaintPlex edge sealer, 250 g](https://www.hornbach.nl/p/paintplex-randsealer-250-gr/10074861/)  
  Edge sealer for wooden sheet material.

The edge sealer should first be tested together with the selected stain. Because the stain is transparent, the sealer may remain visible through the final finish.

## Preferred Stain

- [Hornbach — Hybrid wood stain, silver grey](https://www.hornbach.nl/p/hornbach-hybride-houtbeits-zilvergrijs-750-ml/5509566/)  
  Transparent silver-grey wood stain. Two coats are intended for a new finish.

The wood grain may remain visible, which is acceptable for this project.

## Test Option

The stain is also available in a smaller 375 ml tin.

This makes it possible to test the complete finishing process on an offcut of the actual Okoumé plywood before buying the larger quantity.

The test should include:

1. Sanding the surface and sawn edges
2. Applying edge sealer to the sawn edge
3. Applying the first coat of silver-grey stain
4. Allowing it to dry
5. Applying a second coat

The test should show:

- Whether the silver-grey colour is suitable
- How much of the wood grain remains visible
- How evenly the Okoumé plywood absorbs the stain
- Whether two coats provide an acceptable result
- Whether the edge sealer remains visible

## Estimated Surface Area

The provisional enclosure dimensions are:

- Width: 900 mm
- Height: 450 mm
- Depth: 100 mm

When all six wooden panels are finished on both sides, the estimated surface area is approximately:

| Parts | Area per coat |
|---|---:|
| Front and rear panels | 1.62 m² |
| Top and bottom panels | 0.36 m² |
| Side panels | 0.18 m² |
| **Total** | **2.16 m²** |

For two coats:

- 4.32 m² without reserve
- Approximately 5.2 m² including a 20% allowance

The actual area may be lower if part of the front consists of plexiglass or an open display frame.

## Proposed Process

1. Prepare a test piece of the actual Okoumé plywood.
2. Seal the sawn edge.
3. Apply two coats of silver-grey stain.
4. Evaluate the colour, coverage and visibility of the edge sealer.
5. Use the same process for the complete enclosure if the result is acceptable.
