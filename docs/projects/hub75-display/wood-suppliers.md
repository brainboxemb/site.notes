# Okoumé Wood Suppliers

This page contains suppliers of Okoumé plywood for the HUB75 LED matrix display project.

> Prices were checked in July 2026 and may change over time.
>
> This first overview focuses mainly on quarter-size sheets of approximately 61 × 125 cm.
>
> A separate overview of full-size sheets may be added later, including suppliers that can cut the material to size.

## Surface Types

The listed Okoumé plywood products are available with different surface finishes.

### Unprimed

Natural wood surface without primer or foil.

### Primed

Factory-applied primer intended as a base for paint.

### Paintable Foil

A smooth, paintable foil applied to one or both sides of the plywood.

Sawing or drilling exposes the untreated wood underneath, so edges still require separate finishing.
