# Okoumé Wood Suppliers

This page contains suppliers of Okoumé plywood for the HUB75 LED matrix display project.

> Prices were checked in July 2026 and may change over time.

## Surface Finish Considerations

The available Okoumé plywood products have different surface finishes.

### Unprimed Plywood

Unprimed plywood has a natural wood surface without a factory-applied primer or foil.

This provides the most flexibility for the finishing process. It can be finished with a transparent or coloured stain, allowing the wood grain to remain visible.

Possible considerations:

- The surface may absorb stain unevenly.
- Sanding may be required before finishing.
- Sawn edges require separate treatment.
- More finishing work may be required than with a prepared surface.

### Primed Plywood

Primed plywood has a factory-applied primer intended as a base for paint.

This can reduce the preparation needed for an opaque painted finish.

Possible considerations:

- It is suitable for opaque paint rather than transparent stain.
- The factory-applied primer may reduce the number of paint coats required.
- Sawn edges still require separate treatment.
- The compatibility with the selected finishing product should be checked.

### Plywood with Paintable Foil

Some products from Klusloods and Praxis have a paintable foil on the top and bottom surfaces.

The grey-and-white versions have:

- Grey paintable foil on one side
- White paintable foil on the other side

The grey versions have grey paintable foil on both sides.

Possible advantages:

- Smooth and uniform surface
- Intended to be painted
- Less surface preparation may be required
- A grey or white base can be selected to suit the final colour

Possible considerations:

- The surface is foil rather than untreated wood.
- It is not suitable when a transparent stain is preferred.
- Sawing and drilling expose the untreated wood beneath the foil.
- Sawn edges still require separate treatment.
- Damage to the foil may remain visible in the final finish.

For the current silver-grey stain option, unprimed Okoumé appears to be the most suitable material because the stain can be applied directly to the natural wood surface.

## Current Preference

Unprimed Okoumé currently appears to be the preferred option.

Reasons:

- Compatible with the proposed transparent silver-grey stain
- No factory-applied foil
- Natural wood surface can be tested directly with the selected finish
- Lower price than most foil-covered alternatives

The 10 mm and 18 mm blank sheets from Houthandel van Deuveren are currently among the more affordable options.
